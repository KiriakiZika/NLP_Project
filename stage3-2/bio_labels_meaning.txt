O (Outside)
This word is NOT important for structure.
Examples:
verbs
filler words
grammar words

2. B-OBJ (Beginning of Object)
First word of the object being moved

3. I-OBJ (Inside Object)
Continuation of the object name

4. B-DEST (Beginning of Destination)
First word of where the object is going

5. I-DEST (Inside Destination)
Continuation of the destination

6. B-REL (Relation)
Words that describe HOW the object is placed
Examples:
on
in
under
onto
